Instructions

Welcome to the ONR-ISR Radio Communication Panel. Please follow these steps to operate the radio:

  1. Select the appropriate callsign from the dropdown list.
  2. Choose the radio channel (NAV1, NAV2, COMM1, COMM2) for communication.
  3. Adjust the frequency using the +/- buttons or enter it manually.
  4. Click on 'Transmit' or 'Receive' to communicate.
  5. Close the panel using the 'X' button at the top-right corner.
